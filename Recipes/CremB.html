Crème Brûlée



Discover the exquisite pleasure of Crème Brûlée, where smooth and creamy vanilla-infused custard harmonizes with a perfectly caramelized sugar crust. This French dessert masterpiece is an indulgence that delights the senses, and you'll be amazed at how easily you can create this culinary sensation at home

Ingredients

  • 1 cup heavy cream

  • 1 cup whole milk

  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar

  • 1 vanilla bean or 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

  • 6 large egg yolks

  • Pinch of salt

  • Extra granulated sugar for caramelizing on top

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, combine 1 cup heavy cream, 1/2 cup granulated sugar, and 1 vanilla bean (or 1 tsp vanilla extract). Heat until it simmers, then remove from heat..

  2. In a bowl, whisk 6 egg yolks. Slowly pour the warm cream mixture into the yolks while whisking..

  3. Pour the mixture into ramekins and bake at 325°F (160°C) for 30-40 minutes, until set but slightly jiggly.

  4. Sprinkle sugar on top and caramelize with a torch or broiler.
